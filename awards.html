Awards



We want you to feel that we are a safe pair of hands. We have won many awards since we were founded almost a century ago in 1919.

What we are most proud of is the positive feedback from our students and customers: 98% of City Lit students rate us as excellent, very good or good - it's why so many of our students come back to City Lit year after year.

Here are a selection of our most recent awards:



Ofsted: Grade 1 (Outstanding) for the college.

Investors in People Gold award: demonstrating commitment to staff development.

Beacon status: awarded for the high quality of our courses

Queen's Anniversary Prize: awarded for our world-class speech therapy programme

Matrix accreditation: the quality standard for our advice and guidance service
