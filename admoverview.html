Admission Overview


REGISTRATION PROCEDURE


Candidates desirous of getting into the School need to fill the Admission Form which can be downloaded from this site or available at the School. Free, expert counseling is also available. Candidates below the age of 16 will need to submit their guardian’s/parent’s concurrence to the admission. Outstations Candidates can register, in advance, online or at the School, for programmes already announced or proposed at a future date.


RULES & REGULATIONS


Together with the filled up application form, a student is expected to attach his proof of age. The School would at all times maintain a friendly yet decent code of discipline . Aberrations will not be condoned);
All successful applicants are expected to join the advertised programmes on indicated due dates. Relaxation will be given only in exceptional cases at the discretion of the School authorities.
'Fees/Advance once paid will not be refunded or adjusted in any way whatsoever, under any circumstances'.
Cheques/DDs should be in the name of local Franchisees.


FEES


Fees, as applicable for each of the courses to be paid in advance. Reasonable & convenient installment facilities available.
At the time of Admission: 10% of the course fees, subject to a minimum of Rs 1000/-;
All fees will be paid through bank drafts, cheques & RTGS
